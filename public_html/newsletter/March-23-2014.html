Electioneering

The M@R Newsletter - March 23, 2014


The end of March is upon us, and freedom is so close we can taste it. But Musicians@Ryerson isn’t slowing down just yet – we have a few more exciting events up our sleeve before we start gearing down for the year.


We’d like to thank everyone who came out to watch The Battle of the Bands competition last week – the event was a huge success! And for those who couldn’t make it, we’d like to announce once again that The Lifers were our winners. Congrats, guys! Check out links to their music in the bar down below.

M@R Executive Election Meeting

March 24 - 6:30PM @ the Layton Room

On Monday March 24 in the Layton Room, you’ll get the chance to vote for who you want to represent Musicans@Ryerson for the 2014-2015 school year during our M@R Executive Election Meeting! The event starts at 6:30 PM and we’ll have free pizza, so don’t miss out - who knows, you may even find yourself standing in the presence of M@R’s future president.

Weekly Wednesday Open Mic Night!

Wednesdays 8PM @ The Ram in the Rye

And as always, our Open Mic Night will be taking place on Wednesday in the Ram in the Rye at 8 PM! Whether you want to perform or just take a much needed break from school to hear some live music, we promise that you’ll have a great time. We’ll have a guitar, a keyboard, and a djembe there, but feel free to bring your own instruments!

Have a great week!

Musically Yours,
- The M@R Team
