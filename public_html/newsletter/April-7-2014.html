Never Can Say Goodbye

The M@R Newsletter - April 7, 2014

Congrats everyone – we made it! Soon the 2013-2014 Ryerson school year will be officially over, and in just a couple of weeks we’ll finally be able to sit back, relax, and catch up on sleep. Unfortunately, the end of the school year also means the end of the Musicians@Ryerson year. (Cue ugly sobbing) But in true M@R fashion, we’re ending this year off with a bang.

It’s been an amazing few months everyone. Thank you so much for supporting and being a part of Musicians@Ryerson, and we’re looking forward to seeing all of you next year. And without further ado, for the last time until September:

Have a great week!

Musically Yours,
- The M@R Team

Women in Art

April 8 - 6:00PM-9:00PM @ the Thomas Lounge

You know who doesn’t get enough credit? Awesome lady artists – we should totally do something to fix that. Wait… what’s that? Musicians@Ryerson is helping to hold a Women in Art event on Tuesday April 8 at 6 PM? In the Thomas Lounge? Featuring Ryerson’s best female filmmakers, musicians, and photographers? And it’s going to be FREE? Life is beautiful. Girls and guys alike are welcome to attend.

Weekly Wednesday Open Mic Night!

Wednesdays 8PM @ The Ram in the Rye

Come one, come all to the very last Open Mic Night of the year! As usual, it will be taking place on Wednesday in the Ram in the Rye at 8 PM. Whether you want to perform or just take a much needed break from school to hear some live music, we promise that you’ll have a great time. We’ll have a guitar, a keyboard, and a djembe there, but feel free to bring your own instruments. And since it’s the last one, we had better see all of your beautiful faces there.
