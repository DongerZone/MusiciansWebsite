Cool Instruments and Open Mic Tuesdays!

The M@R Newsletter - January 14, 2013


Welcome back to school! From all of us at M@R, we hope you've had an amazing break and that you're ready for an incredible semester. And we're not talking about fun classes and exciting assignments.

M@R has some really cool events coming up that you do not want to miss out on! Including the weekly open mics, a piano workshop, battle of the bands, and much, much more, this is going to be a semester to remember. Check out more details in the first edition of our redesigned newsletter below!

See you soon!
- The M@R Team

Campus Caravan

Jan. 15 @ Student Center Lobby

This is your second chance to check out and get involved with 80+ RSU student groups. At the M@R table, we'll have a selection of cool instruments to check out, and (most importantly) free stuff!! Come hear about all of our awesome events coming up this semester! Click here for more details.

The Open Mics Are Back!

Tuesdays 2-4 PM @ Ram in the Rye

Your favourite M@R open mics return this semester at a new time! Starting January 22, you'll have the chance to come hang out every week at the Ram to share your musical skills. Email music@ryerson.ca to guarantee a spot!

Jazz Video Challenge

Want the chance to play live with jazz pianist Ron Davis? We're bringing him for a workshop on February 6, and we're giving M@R members the exclusive opportunity to perform a song with him! Just record a 5 minute video of your jazz skills and post it as a response to this video. More details here.

Battle of the Bands

Your chance to shine on stage in front of your fans is here! We're happy to announce that we're having a battle of the bands on January 30th! More details to follow, but if you want to get on the listing, email music@ryerson.ca (1) your band name, (2) genre, and (3) a link to your music!
Bands must have at least one Ryerson member to enter.

Artist Directory

Bands and Solo Artists: The Artist Directory is a great place on our website to discover local artists and promote your own music (for FREE!). Featuring short bios, links to music, and more, this list is often used to find artists for shows and events. If you'd like to get on the directory, fill out this form. (Note: At least one member of your band must be a Ryerson student.)

Find-A-Bandmate

Need a bandmate? Want to join a band? Find-A-Bandmate is a listing on our website of our musicians looking to be part of new projects. No matter what instrument, genre, or skill level, this is the place to complete that band destined for greatness! To sign up, fill out this form. (Note: Must be a current Ryerson student.)
