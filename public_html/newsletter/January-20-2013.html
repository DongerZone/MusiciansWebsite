Do you like Open Mics?

The M@R Newsletter - January 20, 2013


Ever feel like you don't get enough opportunities to play music on campus? Well, not anymore! We're excited to begin our weekly open mics at the Ram this week. These will run every Tuesday through the entire semester. But if that's not enough, we are also partnering with the RSU for a second open mic this week, on Wednesday in Oakham Cafe!

That's right. Back-to-back open mics this week. Tuesday 2-4PM and Wednesday 7:30-10PM. Keep reading for more details on those and other cool things coming up soon (like tomorrow being the last day to submit for the Jazz Video Challenge)!

Musically Yours,
- The M@R Team

The M@R Weekly Open Mics

Tuesdays 2-4 PM @ Ram in the Rye

Ready for the first open mic of the semester? The mics start this Tuesday, January 22. Come hang out with your musician friends and show off your skills! Email music@ryerson.ca to guarantee a spot.

RSU's Open Mic Coffee House

Wednesday, Jan 23, 7:30-10PM @ Oakham House

One open mic per week is not always enough. M@R is partnering with the RSU for the first Coffee House of the year! Arrive at Oakham Cafe around 7pm for sign-up. Music will begin right at 7:30pm. *Coffee and Tea is FREE!

Jazz Video Challenge

Want the chance to play live with jazz pianist Ron Davis? Just record a 5 minute video of your jazz skills and post it as a response to this video. COMPETITION ENDS TOMORROW. More details here.

Journalists / Writers Needed

Want your articles on the M@R website? Introducing the Artist Spotlight, a regular online article series featuring some of our very talented musicians on campus, including interviews, bios, links to music, and maybe even videos. Writers of all skill levels are welcome to join this great initiative to bring more exposure to Ryerson artists. Interested? Email music@ryerson.ca.

Battle of the Bands

Bands and Solo Artists! Our battle has been postponed to February 27th**! More details to follow, but we're still accepting submissions! To enter, email music@ryerson.ca (1) your band name, (2) genre, and (3) a link to your music! Bands must have at least one Ryerson member to enter.

Artist Directory

Want free exposure? The Artist Directory is a great place on our website to discover local artists and promote your own music (for FREE!). Featuring short bios, links to music, and more, this list is often used to find artists for shows and events. If you'd like to get on the directory, fill out this form. (Note: At least one member of your band must be a Ryerson student.)
