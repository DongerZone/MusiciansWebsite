Dark Side of the Moon

The M@R Newsletter - November 11 2014

As the threat of exams looms closer, it’s more important than ever to try to find the right balance between work and fun – and M@R is here to help! Take a break from all that studying to come down to one or both of our amazing events this week – you’ll be able stop yourself from going crazy with stress and giving yourself a coffee-induced coma. (You’re welcome in advance)

Have a great week!

Musically Yours,
- The M@R Team

Darkness Concert

November 12 – 7:00PM @ RCC 204

The 2nd Annual Darkness Concert is finally here! On November 12 (this Wednesday) you’ll be able to hear five amazing acts perform – in complete darkness! Sound cool? Of course it does! It only costs $5 to get in, and all proceeds from the event will go to the Canadian National Institute for the Blind. Drop by RCC 204 at 7 PM to have fun and support a good cause – buy your tickets now!

Open Mic: Unplugged Edition

November 14 – 7:00PM- 10:00PM @ The Riel Room

On a cold November night, sometimes all you want to do is curl up with a cup of coffee, sit down, and listen to something soft and acoustic. That’s why this Friday, we’ve decided to organize an Open Mic Night that’s completely Unplugged! We’ll be bringing along an acoustic guitar, a ukulele, and a djembe for yoy to use, but feel free to bring your own instruments as well! Be at the Riel Room at 7 PM on November 14 or be square.

Also, click here to check out our video on Spotlight artist Charlotte Fabro!
