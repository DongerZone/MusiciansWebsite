The Weekly Open Mics Begin!

The M@R Newsletter - September 8, 2013


Week of Welcome may be over, but the awesome events definitely are not! The famous Weekly Open Mics are back, we've got a cool Meet & Greet on Thursday, and there's tons more on the way! We're working on some really big events, so stay tuned for more details!!

For all our new members (and some old), make sure you join our Facebook group. It's the best place to connect with other members and stay up to date on upcoming M@R events. And don't forget to spread the word to all your friends!

Musically Yours,
- The M@R Team

First M@R Weekly Open Mic of the Semester

Every Tuesday 2-4PM @ Ram in the Rye

Your favourite weekly open mics are BACK! Come perform a few songs or just chill with some cool musicians and listen to great music! We'll have our guitar and djembe there, but feel free to bring your own instruments! Email music@ryerson.ca to guarantee yourself a spot.

Office Warming Meet & Greet

Thursday Sept. 12, 2-4PM @ SCC-B13 (basement of Student Centre)

Want to meet tons of other Ryerson musicians? Want to check out our brand new office? Well you can do BOTH AT THE SAME TIME!! We're hosting a Meet & Greet in the lounge beside our office in the basement of the Student Centre. Drop in between 2-4pm! Free drinks will be provided!

Scope Radio Launch Party

Tuesday, September 10, 5-9pm @ 55 Gould St. Suite 201.Celebrate a new student media space at Ryerson with fresh sounds and good feels. Click here for more details.
