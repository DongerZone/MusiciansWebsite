November Rain

The M@R Newsletter - September 13th, 2016

Welcome back Ryerson students! It’s a brand new year, which means that Musicians@Ryerson has a brand new set of events planned. Concerts, workshops, Open Jams, Open Mics – you name it, we’ve got it. Keep reading to see what Musicians@Ryerson has planned for September. We hope you enjoy the rest of the month and the rest of the semester!

Musically Yours,
- The M@R Team

Hillel 90's Throwback Thursday

September 15 – 5:00 to 7:00PM @ Hillel Loft

Feeling nostalgic for the times when all you had to worry about was whether to watch Scooby Doo or Rugrats? Musicians@Ryerson is collaborating with Hillel Ryerson to organize a 90s Throwback Thursday Event! On Thursday September 15 at 5 PM, come to the Hillel Loft on 21 Dundas Square to eat good food, enjoy good music, and reminisce about the good ‘ol days.

SLC Open Jam

September 20 – 3:00 to 6:00PM @ SLC Lobby

Going back to school means the return of Open Jams! Our very first one will be held in the SLC Lobby. Join us on September 20 any time from 3 to 6 PM to try out new instruments, meet new people, and have fun!

Weekly Open Mics

Every Wednesday – 8:00 PM @ Ram in the Rye

Weekly Open Mic Night continues! Join us at the Ram in the Rye every Wednesday night at 8:00 PM to join a friend on stage for an acoustic duet, test out an original song, or sit back and enjoy the performances. As always, M@R will be able to supply you with a few instruments, but feel free to bring your own as well!

Band Watch

Ryerson is full of talented musicians, and many of them are members of M@R! Check out this article from The Eyeopener to learn more about Ryerson’s best up-and-coming bands.

Find a Bandmate

The Find-A-Bandmate page on our website has recently been updated! Check it out if you want to form a band or just find cool people to play music with.

Follow us!

Stay up to date with M@R on Twitter and Instagram @RyeMusicians, and on our Facebook page. Tell your friends!
