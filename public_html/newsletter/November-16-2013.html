FREE GUITAR LESSONS

The M@R Newsletter - November 16, 2013


Did you catch us at CultureJAM last week? We put together a large group of extremely talented musicians, and in about a month put together our own 90s medley! If you missed it, we've found a video with decent cell-phone quality! (Actual videography wasn't allowed at the event). Watch it here!

November is half way done, but we've got sooooooo many incredible events left! Guitar Lessons, two concerts (one in pitch black!) and of course, our weekly Open Mics from 2-4PM! Don't miss out!

Musically Yours,
- The M@R Team

Free Guitar Lessons

Monday, Nov. 18, 6-8PM @ Multifaith Room (111 Gerrard St E)

Is your guitar just sitting around the house collecting dust? Now is the perfect time to pick it up put it to great use! Free guitar lessons are going to be held by M@R student volunteer teachers. We will cover most of the basics to start your way into greatness:

- Chords
- Theory
- Notation

More info here.

M@R Weekly Open Mics

Every Tuesday 2-4PM @ Ram in the Rye

Come perform a few songs or just chill with some cool musicians and listen to great music! We'll have our guitar and djembe there, but feel free to bring your own instruments! Post here to guarantee yourself a spot.

Beaudifulhors with M@R CONCERT

Saturday, Nov. 23, 8:30PM @ Annette Studios (566 Annette St)

Come out to this great show, featuring an awesome line up of M@R artists! Here's how things will be going down:

9 PM - 9:20 PM : Mahan Bahari
9:30 PM- 10:10 PM : Kopano
10:20 PM - 11 PM : RUJazz
11:10 PM - 12 AM : beaudifulhors

Doors will open at 8:30 PM. $5 tickets will be available (email music@ryerson.ca or any of the acts for the hook up) or you can opt for the $7 door cover. A night with all kinds of beaudiful music, you don't want to miss it! More info.

DARKNESS: Music for Dreams

Tuesday, Nov. 26, 7:30-9PM @ Oakham Lounge (Oakham House)

Held in complete darkness, this concert aims to give the audience the unique opportunity to experience music like never before.

Featuring talented Ryerson artists!
Doors open at 7PM.
Admission: $5, with all proceeds towards Canadian National Institute for the Blind (CNIB), supporting the visually impaired community.

In collaboration with the Chinese Students Association and CNIB. More info.
