Learn How Your Music Can Make Money!

The M@R Newsletter - February 2, 2014


Hey everyone,
Thank you for those of you who came out last week to our open mic and Welcome Back Winter Bash! Both events were a great success and we had a hoot.

This Tuesday we have another workshop for all musicians and artists. This one covers how to make money by making music. Then of course we have our weekly open mic on Wednesday.

Hope to see you there!

Musically Yours,
- The M@R Team

Musicians Workshop 2: Revenue Streams

Tuesday 6:30PM-8PM @ Layton Room (55 Gould St.)

We’re back with another workshop! Calling all artists and musicians - discover new ways to capitalize on your talent and make money making music! Stephen Foster, Director of Artist and Label Services at EBA Inc., will return to answer your questions about bringing home the bacon, along with EBA Ince Royalties Manager, Mikal Gonzales.

Weekly Wednesday Open Mic Night!

Wednesdays 8PM @ The Ram in the Rye

Come perform a few songs or just chill with some cool musicians and listen to great music! We'll have our guitar and djembe there, but feel free to bring your own instruments!
