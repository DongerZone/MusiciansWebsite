Services

Our goal is to provide as much music-related activities for students as possible and we do so by offering a variety of fun music events for students to do on campus!

Open Mics

We host open mics at the Ram-in-the-Rye every Wednesday from 8:30pm-11:30pm. We also provide provide instruments such as a steel-string guitar, djembe, ukulele, bass guitar, shakers, and often a keyboard.

Open Jams

Imagine instruments spread out and anyone can grab one and jam! Open Jams are an exciting way for students to jump in, rock and meet other musicians!

Free Music Lessons

We give free music lessons! Students can come and learn from out team or teach one another to play an instrument. We offer lessons for guitar, ukelele and keys!
