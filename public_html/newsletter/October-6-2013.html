EPIC MUSIC

The M@R Newsletter - October 6, 2013


Who doesn't love it? Especially in movies. So we're going to chill on Thursday and listen to some classics! What better way to get you in the mood for midterm studying. And, of course, don't forget the Weekly Open mic on Tuesday 2-4PM at the Ram.

There are opportunities for you to show off your cool band and (for those of you that are First Years) to join the M@R Exec! See below for details. And here's a random link to tell you to join our Facebook page.

Musically Yours,
- The M@R Team

Epic Movie Soundtrack Night

Thursday, Oct. 10 @ 7PM @ SCC201

You like music, but do you like it in film? Come this Thursday to chill out with your fellow musicians and listen to and talk about your favourite movie scores. There'll be snacks and good people! Post songs for the playlist here.

M@R Weekly Open Mics

Every Tuesday 2-4PM @ Ram in the Rye

Come perform a few songs or just chill with some cool musicians and listen to great music! We'll have our guitar and djembe there, but feel free to bring your own instruments! Post here to guarantee yourself a spot.
