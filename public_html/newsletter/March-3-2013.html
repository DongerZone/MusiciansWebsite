Music, Mind and Technology

The M@R Newsletter - March 3, 2013


Isn't it time you learned something interesting at school? Aside from our riveting curriculums, of course. Well, this week, aside from our usual Tuesday Open Mics at the Ram (2-4PM), we're having a workshop with Ryerson's SMART lab that will do just that! More info below.

And for those of you who couldn't make it to the Battle of the Bands at The Central last week, we're proud to announce the winner... Dante Matas!! Thanks to all the extremely talented bands and to all who came out and made such a successful event!

Music, Mind and Technology

Thursday, March 7, 6-7:30PM @ SBB238

Come to an awesome workshop on an exciting research field. Led by the Science of Music, Auditory Research and Technology (SMART) lab, this is bound to be one cool workshop! More info here!

M@R Exec Nominations Open!

Until March 8 at Midnight

Want to join the M@R Exec team? Nominations are officially open for the 2013/2014 year. Available positons are: (1) President, (2) Vice President, (3) Performance Chair, (4) Social Chair, (5) Treasurer, (6) Promotions and Marketing Chair. Interested? Email our CRO Elliott for more info and a nomination form!

Ryerson's Next Top Speaker

March 7, 5PM in the Sears Atrium (ENG). The annual speaking competition hosted by Ryerson Toastmasters. The event features live music by M@R's own jazz ensemble!! Check it out here.

Unconventional Instruments

March 12, 6PM in SCC115. We're holding an event with completely unconventional instruments (pots, pans, spoons, kazoos, melodica's, balloons). It's basically going to be really experimental- a big random jam session! Info here.
