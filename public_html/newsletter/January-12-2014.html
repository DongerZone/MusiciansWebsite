Welcome Back!

The M@R Newsletter - January 12, 2014


Welcome back everyone! We hope you all had a relaxing much-needed break. We’ve got some great events planned for this upcoming term and we hope to see all your pretty faces there!

UPDATE: You talked, we listened! Due to much request, we have changed the time and date of our Weekly Open Mic to better accommodate students’ schedules. They will now be on Wednesdays at the Ram in the Rye from 8PM-10PM starting on January 29th.

Check out below for the details on two events happening this Tuesday.

Musically Yours,
- The M@R Team

Campus Groups Day

Tuesday, Jan 14, 11AM-3PM @ SCC115

Come check us out at Campus Groups Day! We don’t bite. You can meet other musicians, hear what’s in store for this semester and get some free swag. There may also be a six-foot guitar. You heard it right, come see it for yourself! You can join the Facebook group here - https://www.facebook.com/events/195682990639340/

Welcome Back Open Mic

5PM-8PM @Thomas Lounge

Looking to perform or hear some tunes? Grab a bite and beer from the menu or enjoy some complimentary snacks at our Welcome Back Open Mic. We'll have our guitar and djembe there, but feel free to bring your own instruments! If you’ve got something to share we want to hear it! Did we mention there will be snacks?
