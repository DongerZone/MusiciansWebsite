400 Facebook Members!!

The M@R Newsletter - September 15, 2013


That is QUITE the accomplishment! But you know what would make that even better? 500 members! If you haven't already, make sure you join our Facebook group. And invite all your friends!! (Well, at least the musical ones that go to Ryerson...)

We've got some really cool (free) events this week planned out just to celebrate. From jamming on the street, to the Tuesday open mics, to an awesome pub night, we guarantee you a good time this week - or your money back!

Musically Yours,
- The M@R Team

Busking! Open Jam on Gould

Monday Sept. 16, 3-5PM @ Lake Devo (Gould and Victoria)

Come jam with us outside on Monday afternoon, 3-5pm. We'll have some of our instruments there, but feel free to bring your own! Click here for more info.

Fall Kickoff Party!

Thursday Sept. 19, 6PM @ Lou Dawg's (76 Gerrard St. E.)

Musicians@Ryerson's Kickoff Party is here! We'll be having a few acoustic sets by our very own artists. It'll be a great way for everyone to meet and talk in a relaxed and fun setting. Who knows - maybe you'll find people who will potentially be your bandmates! It is ALL AGES and there is NO COVER CHARGE. Just come in and enjoy yourselves! Click here for more info.

M@R Weekly Open Mics

Every Tuesday 2-4PM @ Ram in the Rye

Come perform a few songs or just chill with some cool musicians and listen to great music! We'll have our guitar and djembe there, but feel free to bring your own instruments! Email music@ryerson.ca to guarantee yourself a spot.

Guitar Workshop

Monday, Sept 23, 6PM @ JOR440. Pros Paul Swoger-Ruston, Nic Ladouceur and Jeremy Bellaviti host a group discussion on ways to develop an individually tailored and efficient practice routine. More details.

Journalists / Media Needed

Want your articles on the M@R website? Writers, photographers, videographers of all skill levels are welcome to join the Artist Spotlight team. Interested? Email music@ryerson.ca.
