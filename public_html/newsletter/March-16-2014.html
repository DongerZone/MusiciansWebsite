We Will Rock You

The M@R Newsletter - March 16, 2014


It might be hard to believe, but Spring is almost upon us! And even though the much needed promise of warmer weather is definitely making us happy, Musicians@Ryerson is finding more than one reason to celebrate this week.

Battle of the Bands

Thursday March 20 - 6:00PM @ the Imperial Pub

The time has come, everyone! On March 20th, we’ll finally be holding our Battle of the Bands competition at the Imperial Pub at 6 PM. It’ll be a night of awesome music, great food, and an all-around good time – and all for the low, low price of $4! Come out to watch DiverseCity, Little Boxers, Searching for Kim and more compete for the prize.

Weekly Wednesday Open Mic Night!

Wednesdays 8PM @ The Ram in the Rye

And as always, our Open Mic Night will be taking place on Wednesday in the Ram in the Rye at 8 PM! Whether you want to perform or just take a much needed break from school to hear some live music, we promise that you’ll have a great time. We’ll have a guitar, a keyboard, and a djembe there, but feel free to bring your own instruments!

Have a great week!

Musically Yours,
- The M@R Team
