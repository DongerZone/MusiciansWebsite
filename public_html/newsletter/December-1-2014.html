‘Till We Meet Again

The M@R Newsletter - December 1 2014

It might sound too good to be true, but first semester is almost over! Unfortunately, with the end of tests, exams, and assignments comes the end of M@R events – for now, at least. Before we say goodbye, we wanted to give you a preview of the amazing workshops, concerts, and meet-ups that we’re planning for next semester – scroll down and take a look! Also, make sure to stay tuned to the M@R Facebook page over the break for more updates and announcements.

Thanks for being awesome, and we’ll see everyone next year!

Musically Yours,
- The M@R Team

Battle of the Bands

Mid-March

That’s right, Battle of the Bands is back and better than ever! The date for Battle of the Bands has yet to be announced, but keep checking the M@R Facebook page for updates so you can make sure to get those video auditions finished and uploaded! Stay tuned for more news about the Big Prize we have planned for this year’s BoB winners as well!

Singer-Songwriter Workshop

February 23

Ever dream about being the next Leonard Cohen? Want to one day follow in Taylor Swift’s footsteps? (Who doesn’t?) Then be sure to come to our special Singer-Songwriter Workshop next semester! On Monday February 23, we’ll be having an expert come in to reveal all of the best-kept song writing secrets.

Open Jams

All Semester

If you wanted to go to the Open Jam this semester but couldn’t make it, have no fear! Starting in January we’ll be having FIVE Open Jams throughout the semester, so you’ll have more chances to meet and collaborate with fellow M@R members! We’ll be having our first one on January 22 – make sure to look out for room details and more!
