Mmmmm... Elections...

The M@R Newsletter - March 17, 2013


It's time to pick your execs for next years' M@R board! On Monday, we're having a pizza night (free!) to give you a chance to meet the nominees. Just in time for voting, starting on Tuesday! Check the Facebook page frequently this week for a link to the online voting system.

And as always, come hang out at our weekly Open Mic! Tuesdays 2-4PM at the Ram!

Musically Yours,
- The M@R Team
