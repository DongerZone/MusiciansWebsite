Back in Black

The M@R Newsletter - February 23, 2014


Yep, you read the title right, we’re back in black – because like many of you, we’re mourning the end of our short-lived reading week. But on the bright, side, another week of school means another week of amazing Musicians@Ryerson events!

On Monday we have the second installment of our FREE Guitar Lessons! Never picked up a guitar in your life? Not a problem. Been buried in piles of homework and it’s been months since you’ve practiced? That’s cool too. Come to the Layton Room in the Oakham House from 6 to 8 PM and we’ll teach you or refresh your memory on everything you need to know.

And as always, feel free to attend our Weekly Open Mic on Wednesday at 8 PM at the Ram in the Rye. You can sing, beatbox, or maybe even show off your newfound guitar skills. (Because let’s face it – those free guitar lessons are just too good to pass up)

Oh, and by the way – check out this promotional video put together by the Musicians@Ryerson team by clicking here!

Have a great week!

Musically Yours,
- The M@R Team
