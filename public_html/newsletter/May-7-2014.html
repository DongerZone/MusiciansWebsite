Thanks for the Memories

The M@R Newsletter - May 7, 2014

Hey everyone! Now that we’re all done with exams, we just thought that we’d send a final newsletter looking back on the year that was.

In addition to our usual weekly Open Mic Nights and regular Guitar Workshops we held throughout the year, we also had lots of bigger, even more exciting events.

Remember the DARKNESS: Music for Dreams concert we had back in November to raise money for the Canadian National Institute for the Blind? Or the Battle of the Bands competition we had in March? Or the Big 500 semiformal we had to celebrate the fact that the M@R Facebook page added its 500th member? It’s hard to believe, but right now we’re already at over 700 members! A few months – and a few members – can make a huge difference. So next year we’re definitely going to try to repay you guys for all of your support by inviting you to events that are just as, if not more, amazing than the ones we had this year.

And without further ado… the big news!

That’s right – now when we call ourselves the RSU Student Group of the Year 2013-2014, it’ll finally be official! Congrats to M@R president Eli Vandersluis, Vice President Rebecca Ortiz, and the rest of the M@R team for making the 2013-2014 year such a successful one.

Once again, we can’t thank you guys enough for supporting us this year. We’re going to try our best to make next year even better, and hopefully you’ll be there to take the ride with us!

Have a great summer!

Musically Yours,

- The M@R Team
