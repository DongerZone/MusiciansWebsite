The Winner Takes It All

The M@R Newsletter - February 16, 2014


Ah, Reading Week. A time to temporarily escape the cold weather by travelling to various tropical destinations, doing some hardcore Netflix marathoning, or catching up on much-needed sleep.

And since it’s Reading Week, that sadly means there won’t be an Open Mic this Wednesday. (Cue sobbing) But don’t fret – we still have some good news for you. It’s time to officially announce the winner of our Love Cover Contest! And it is (drumroll please)…

…Jonathan Kim!

Congrats Jonathan! Check out his winning cover of Say Something over on Youtube!

And before we say goodbye to you for a week, we’d like to leave you with this amazing Jamagram from this past week, courtesy of Aarone Amino. Check it out over here!

Have an amazing Reading Week!

Musically Yours,
- The M@R Team
