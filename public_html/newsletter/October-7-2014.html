Did You Miss Me?

The M@R Newsletter - October 7, 2014

Hello M@R members new and old! After a relaxing four month summer break and a maybe-not-so-relaxing first month of school, the M@R newsletter is back! (With a vengeance) Even though we haven’t even finished midterms, we already have a lot of amazing events that we’re planning for this week and the weeks to come.

September was already filled with a couple of Open Mics and our awesome Groove Therapy event at Lou Dawg’s but what’s in store for October? Check out two events coming soon, and stay tuned to the newsletter for more information about our exciting pre-Halloween event taking place on Wednesday October 29!

Have a great week!

Musically Yours,
- The M@R Team

Free Ukulele Lessons!

October 21 - 6:00PM-9:00PM @ the Shadd Room

What’s this? Guitar lessons AND ukulele lessons? Surely this is too good to be true (but nothing is ever too good for us). Back by popular demand, we had a night of Guitar Lessons today from 6 to 9 PM in the Shadd Room. (That’s on the first floor of the Oakham House, for those who don’t know) Then on Tuesday October 21st (same time and place) we’ll be introducing Ukulele Lessons! Whether you’re a guitar player and want to expand your horizons or just think ukuleles are awesome, this event is for you.

Weekly Wednesday Open Mic Night!

Wednesdays 8PM @ The Ram in the Rye

And of course, we wouldn’t be M@R without the Open Mics. We don’t have one this week, but make sure to stop by the Ram in the Rye the Wednesday after reading week from 8 to 11 PM to hear some of Ryerson’s most talented musicians.
