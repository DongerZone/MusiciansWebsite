No Open Mic This Week

The M@R Newsletter - February 17, 2013


Happy Reading Week! From all of us at M@R, we hope you have a great week off full of studying, homework, assignments, and (most of all) relaxing after a busy half-semester.

No Weekly Open Mic at the Ram in the Rye this week, but they will be back in full force on February 26. Also, that week brings the much-anticipated Battle of the Bands on February 27, and another of your favourite busking events on February 28. More details below.

Musically Yours,
- The M@R Team

Reading Week!

February 18-22

No school this week! We know that you needed the reminder. As a result, no Open Mic this week.

The M@R Battle of the Bands!

Wednesday, Feb 27, 7PM
The Central (603 Markham St.)

Come to the first annual Battle of the Bands at The Central, bringing together some of Ryerson University's top musical acts! ALL AGES! Doors open at 6:30PM. More details here.

Indoor Busking!

On February 28, 12-2PM, in the SCC Lobby. Since it's probably going to be cold that day! Bring your own instruments! (BYOI)

M@R Acapella Group

Singers, beatboxers and acapella-ers! We're putting together an acapella group and want YOU to join! Email Maricris for more info and to sign up!

Who's your fave Musician?

Post their picture on the Facebook Group and tell us why he or she is your favourite. Why should you? Well, why not!

Journalists / Writers Needed
