Thanks for a Great Semester!

The M@R Newsletter - November 30, 2013


Seems like Week of Welcome was just yesterday... In only 3 months, we've had such a blast hosting a wide variety of events and programs: The Weekly Open Mics, Office Warming Meet-&-Greet, Busking, Kickoff Party at Lou Dawg's, Guitar Workshop with Paul Ruston-Swoger, Speed Jamming, Epic Movie Soundtrack Night, Monster Mash Karaoke Bash, Guitar Hero Night, "Big 500" Semi Formal, CultureJam Showcase, Free Guitar Lessons, Beaudifulhors & More Concert, the DARKNESS Concert, and much more!

While M@R will not be hosting any events in December (including the Open Mics), we'll be using the time to plan out next semester to be even better! Just to warn you, we've got some really exciting things coming up...

We'd like to take this opportunity to thank you again for being a member of M@R, and to wish you the best of luck during your exams! Only two more weeks before you can take that much-deserved winter break!!

See you in Winter 2014!
- The M@R Team
