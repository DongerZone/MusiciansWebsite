Spotlight Staff Biographies

Editor

Alina Bykova

Alina Bykova is a writer, photographer and videographer majoring in journalism at Ryerson. In her spare time she enjoys looking at architecture, practising yoga and wrestling with her Instagram addiction. You can find samples of her work at http://alinabykova.format.com

Writers

Imani Walker

Always attracted to big cities and the fast life, Imani has recently moved to Toronto where she is in her second year of journalism at Ryerson University. Going to concerts and spoken word poetry slams has become her saving grace. Imani also reports for RuTv News and would like to work for Buzzfeed at least once in her lifetime. If you're in the city and you're lucky, you may catch her running through the 6ix with her woes.

Hannah Polinski

Hannah is a creative writer and world explorer studying English at Ryerson University. When not composing the next great American novel or fricking up the patriarchy she can be found petting small dogs on the streets of Toronto

Kayla Douglas

A music enthusiast, globetrotter, and lover of all things alternative, Kayla is a first-year journalism student at Ryerson University. She prides herself on her ability to determine who is in an indie rock band based on their hairstyle and what shoes they are wearing. She also likes reading and sarcasm. You can find some of her work here: http://frontrv.blogspot.ca/

Diana Whistance-Smith

A third year journalism student, I've been passionate about music as far back as I can remember. I've focused on piano, but have played guitar and trumpet as well. I've been able to put my love for music into writing as well, and hope to continue to do so in my future career.

Justin Bellmore

Justin Bellmore is a second year journalism student at Ryerson University. He was bitten by a travel bug once, and is now permanently infected, determined to visit all corners of the globe. He is a hopelessly optimistic, dog loving, rock enthusiast, and currently finds great difficulty in finding time to spare between his music blog and his protein shakes. http://www.soundleagues.com/blog
