Double-Shot of Open Mics!

The M@R Newsletter - September 29, 2013


There's a lot to tell you this week! We're hosting TWO open mics this Tuesday, just for those of you who can't make the usual 2-4PM time, and we have a great event this Friday 2-4PM to help you meet those bandmates you've always wanted! As well, we've launched the first weekly Artist Spotlight of the year: Beaudifulhors. Also, there are opportunities for singers to join a cool ensemble for a performance, and for those leaders among you to join the M@R exec! More details on all of those below!

Oh, and did you see the October events calendar up on our Facebook page?

Musically Yours,
- The M@R Team

M@R Weekly Open Mics

Every Tuesday 2-4PM @ Ram in the Rye

Come perform a few songs or just chill with some cool musicians and listen to great music! We'll have our guitar and djembe there, but feel free to bring your own instruments! Email music@ryerson.ca to guarantee yourself a spot.

Open Mic Night

Tuesday Oct. 1, 5-8PM @ Thomas Lounge, Oakham House

Tired of missing the totally-awesome Weekly Open Mic? Well we've put together a special open mic at an ALTERNATIVE TIME! As usual, our guitar and djembe will be there. Click here for more details.

Speed Jamming

Friday Oct. 4, 2-4PM @ Multifaith Room (111 Gerrard St. E)

Sometimes, all it takes is a few minutes with another musician to know if the two of you were meant to play together. Introducing Speed Jamming! It's like speed dating, but we'll be pairing people up with different musicians for five minutes of jamming. Bring your own instruments (acoustic please -- or it's gonna get pretty loud in there)! Click here for more details.
