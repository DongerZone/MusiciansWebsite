This is Halloween

The M@R Newsletter - October 25, 2014

It’s the last week of October, and you know what that means – Halloween is just around the corner! And M@R has not just one, but two events this week to help you get yourself into the right frame of mind before you go out on Friday.

Have a great week and an amazing Halloween!

Musically Yours,
- The M@R Team

Free Ukulele Lessons!

October 21 - 6:00PM-9:00PM @ the Shadd Room

FGot back to school Monday and realized how far behind you still are on your homework? Take your mind off of it for a couple hours and come to Ukulele Lessons! Come to the Shadd Room on October 21 at 6 PM and you’ll be well on your way to impressing friends and enemies alike with your mad uke skills. Maybe it’ll even get your creative juices flowing so you can finally finish that essay you were putting off.

Weekly Wednesday Open Mic Night!

Wednesdays 8PM @ The Ram in the Rye

Yes, it’s true – Open Mic Night is finally back this week! We know a bunch of you can’t wait to get back on stage, so make sure to stop by the Ram and the Rye at 8 PM to blow us away with your performance or cheer on fellow M@R members.
