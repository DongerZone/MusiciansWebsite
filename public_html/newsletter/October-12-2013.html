No Open Mic This Week

The M@R Newsletter - October 12, 2013


Reading Week! For all of you lacking the pleasure of being in Engineering, we hope you're ready for the much-awaited week off. We will not be holding any events this week, including our weekly open mic. They will continue again next Tuesday October 22, 2-4pm in the Ram. There are also a lot of other cool events once we get back, so please refer to the Events Calendar on our Facebook page.

Meantime, Our Artist Spotlight is up and running with full force! Check out this week's feature on Maccie Paquette! See below about getting involved with the Spotlight initiative.

Musically Yours,
- The M@R Team
