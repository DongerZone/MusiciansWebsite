Music in Pitch Black

The M@R Newsletter - November 24, 2013


Can you believe there is only one week left of the semester! But don't worry, we have two more solid events happening before winter break (and exams)!

This coming Tuesday may be the best Tuesday of all eternity. Like seriously. First, we have our last open mic of the semester, 2-4PM at the Ram, and it promises to be the biggest party yet! Second (and you want to pay attention for this one), we're hosting a benefit concert in complete darkness, starting at 7PM in Oakham Lounge. This is going to be such an insanely unique and awesome experience that there's no way you can miss it!

Musically Yours,
- The M@R Team

DARKNESS: Music for Dreams

Tuesday, Nov. 26, 7-9PM @ Oakham Lounge (Oakham House)

Held in complete darkness, this concert aims to give the audience the unique opportunity to experience music like never before.

Featuring talented Ryerson artists!
Doors open at 7PM.
Admission: $5, with all proceeds towards Canadian National Institute for the Blind (CNIB), supporting the visually impaired community.

In collaboration with the Chinese Students Association and CNIB. More info.

Last Open Mic of the Semester!

Tuesday 2-4PM @ Ram in the Rye

Come perform a few songs or just chill with some cool musicians and listen to great music! We'll have our guitar and djembe there, but feel free to bring your own instruments! Post here to guarantee yourself a spot.
