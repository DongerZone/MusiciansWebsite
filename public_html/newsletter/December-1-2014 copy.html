It Won’t Be Long

The M@R Newsletter - November 17 2014

Just two more weeks, everyone! (Of classes, at least. Exams are another story.) Whether your homework load is finally starting to die down or the onslaught of tests and assignments has only just begun, M@R is back once again to make your week a little bit better. (Or a lot better. Let’s face it, we’re pretty awesome.) Keep on reading to find out what we have in store!

Have a great week!

Musically Yours,
- The M@R Team

M@R Musical Movie Night ft. Across the Universe

November 18 - 6:30PM-11:30PM @ The Shadd Room

What’s better than an M@R Musical Movie Night? An M@R Musical Movie Night featuring Across the Universe of course! We guarantee it’ll be way better than watching it in a theatre or at home with friends, because singing along is not only permitted, but encouraged! Come to the Shadd Room this Tuesday at 6:30PM to enjoy some classic Beatles music, a great story, and Jim Sturgess’ beautiful, beautiful face.

Open Mic Night

November 19 – 8:00PM- 11:00PM @ The Ram in the Rye

Even though the acoustic Open Mic Night we had last week was undoubtedly awesome, this week we’re going back to basics! Join us at the Ram in the Rye this Wednesday at 8PM for another night of inspiring performances from your fellow M@R members and Ryerson students! As usual, we’ll be bringing along a keyboard, ukulele, bass guitar, acoustic guitar, and djembe for you to use.
