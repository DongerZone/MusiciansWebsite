Who's pumped for September?!

The M@R Newsletter - August 19, 2013


It's hard not to be after so much awesomeness last year! We've got so many cool things in store for you guys this year, including your favourite open mics, workshops, jams, and much, much more! Stay tuned for full details in the next couple weeks.

In the meantime, we just wanted to let you know of some of the things that we accomplished last year and what's new for this one. Thanks again for your ongoing support and enthusiasm in making M@R one of the best student groups at Ryerson!

Musically Yours,
- The M@R Team

Winner of RSU's Best New Student Group 2012-2013!

Thanks to you guys, we had the honour of winning several RSU awards recognizing our success last year. In addition to Best New Student Group, we also won Best Membership Engagement (Runner-Up) and Student Group Partner of the Year (1st Runner-Up)!

Finally, an office you want to be sent to!

Come check out the new M@R HQ: SCC-B13 (Student Campus Centre, 55 Gould St). You can even phone us there at 416-979-5000 x2389. It'll be an awesome place to chill out with execs, meet other members, and play some music!

Stay tuned for office hours.

Brand-spankin'-new Instruments!

We're happy to announce that we now have a Fender plug-in acoustic guitar and a Toca djembe just for YOU! Come try them out in our office, and/or bring your own instruments to jam! And of course, you'll be sure to see them at many of our upcoming events!

LOTS more great events!

It's hard to list all the events that happened last year. The open mics, performances, busks, drum circle, and jazz workshop barely scratch the surface. But there's plenty more where they came from!!

(Have a cool event or program that you'd like to see happen? Email us at music@ryerson.ca!)

Journalists / Writers Needed

Want your articles on the M@R website? Writers of all skill levels are welcome to join the Artist Spotlight. Interested? Email music@ryerson.ca.

M@R Media Team

Photographers, graphic designers, bloggers and more! We're putting together the a media team and we'd love to have you on it! Interested? Email music@ryerson.ca.

The Artist Directory

Want free exposure? The Artist Directory is a great place on our website to discover local artists and promote your own music (for FREE!). Just fill out this form.

The Facebook Group

Have you joined yet? It's the ultimate place for our members. Talk music, share tunes, network, and much more! Invite your friends! facebook.com/groups/ryemusicians.
